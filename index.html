Demographics of Countries around the World


As Legislative Assembly of New Brunswick (2020) mentions it is important to know about the demographics of each country as it affects the economic, social, cultural and politcal aspect of human activity. Knowing the demographics of countries can help understand past trends and help governments prepare for developments and policies. It also allows users to to observe the economic and social trends.

How much money does each country make?

In the choropleth map below, it is shown that most rich countries are coastal countries. According to Business.un.org (n.d.) 90% of the world's trade is maritime. This is in line with Wolla (2017) who states that the main reasons to why countries become rich is through international trading.




How about the birth rate and death rates ?

The bubbleplot shows that most countries with high birth rates would also have high death rates and vice versa with some exceptions. Usually less developed countries have high birth rates and death rates, this is due to the lack of family planning, contraceptives, high infant mortality rates. These reasons are a result of poor health care and infrastructure. (Population Reference Bureau, 2009)

Regions and their mobile phone's distribution

If you look closely at the density distribution of phones and compare it to the GDP (per capita) map above. It can be observed that the GDP and the distribution of phones are positively correlated.
